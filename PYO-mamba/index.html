Home

The black mamba is a long, slender, cylindrical snake. It has a coffin-shaped head with a somewhat pronounced brow ridge and a medium-sized eye. The adult snake's length typically ranges from 2 to 3 meters (6 feet 7 inches to 9 feet 10 inches) but specimens have grown to lengths of 4.3 to 4.5 meters (14 feet 1 inch to 14 feet 9 inches). It is the longest species of venomous snake in Africa and the second-longest venomous snake species overall, exceeded in length only by the king cobra.
